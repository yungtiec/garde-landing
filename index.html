Making public blockchains safe.

A decentralized network of Gardians who support innovators and protect consumers by improving transparency.

How it works
Collaboration and transparency are bedrocks of healthy ecosystems. Our token-powered crypto-economic and bounties systems enable projects and communities to leverage a community of experts — our “Gardians” — to collaborate on and improve transparency on the issues that are most important for consumer tokens
The Brooklyn Project has worked together with industry participants, regulators and policymakers to identify these issues, which focus on the three pillars of transparency, i.e. the completeness, clarity and reliability of disclosures by token projects. You can find more information on these issues, which are now set forth in the Consumer Token Framework

01 04

Introducing Gardecards

Gardecards are an important tool for the Garde Network. They act as scorecards that summarize the level of transparency that projects provide on the issues that The Brooklyn Project and other stakeholders have identified as the most important for token projects. View our current Gardecards for Civil.
02 04

Who are Gardians?

They are the first line of protection -- the “Gardians” -- of the decentralized web. They play two key roles:
(1) create and collaborate on Gardecards, including by fulfilling bounties to review a token project’s transparency practices
(2) curate and verify information on our forthcoming crypto-economic system.
Together, Gardians make up the community of experts that help improve the transparency and safety of the ecosystem.
03 04

Who should apply to be a Gardian?

If you are interested, we would love for you to become a Gardian. We are looking for a diverse set of skills, experiences and areas of expertise. We envision Gardians having backgrounds in software, startups, finance, venture capital and investing, audit, law and regulation, but anyone who shares in our vision of collaborative transparency and regulation is encouraged to sign up.
04 04

What if I want a project to become Garded?

The Garde Network is always expanding. If you are working on or know of a project, you can nominate a project to become “Garded.” Once submitted, interested Gardians can start creating and collaborating on Gardecards for these projects.

How can I get involved?
You can contribute in two ways:
1. sign up to be a Gardian
2. nominate a project to be Garded
Once you sign up as a Gardian, you can view all active bounties to contribute to Gardecards for various token project and start fulfilling these bounties.
